Andrew Kail is an HPC Advisory Scientific Engineer at Data in Science Technologies deploying and managing High Performance Computing systems and is a PhD candidate at the University of Arkansas Little Rock and a researcher in the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. He was previously the scientific application development lead for the Integrated Computing group at Savannah River National Laboratory. There he was also a data scientist, full-stack developer and HPC systems administrator. Before joining SRNL he was a graduate research assistant for the Joint Institute for Computations Sciences (JICS) where he worked on the development of loosely-coupled physics models designed to run at scale.
